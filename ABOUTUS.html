BUGEMA UNIVERSITY VOLLEYBALL CLUB

Bugema university is a charted seventhday founded instition for quality higher learning all around East Africa-Uganda.The club is comprised of the men and the ladies who all adoppted the slogan "lighters"". In the fisrt place the club began with the men's team who played the serie C in the Ugandan volleyball that is prepared and organised by the "UGANDA VOLLEYBALL FEDERATION(UVF)". The club showed great performance over the season and managed to get promoted to Serie B following the fact that the best two teams that finish the season at the table are taken to Serie B. For the case of the ladies("she lighters") its their first appearence in the ladies serie B women's category. Our training schedue is from Monday-Friday and then Sunday if it happens that their no league games at the main campus grounds. Following the fact that it's an adventist institution, our league games are played over sundays. In most cases our games are hosted at either at Nkumba university or MUBS.
