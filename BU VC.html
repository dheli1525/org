WELCOME ABOARD TO HOME OF QUALITY VOLLEYBALL

Bugema university volleyball club welcomes you with alot of pleasure.

This is a club that has got both men and ladies teams that are playing and competing in Ugandan volleyball sport. The was founded by the university its self meaning it owns all the authorities over the amendments towards the the club.Following the required procedures by the federation, a team had to from serie C to play, and in this serie only one team would be promoted to serie B and then the religated team from serie B joins serie C. This means that our journey as men we began from serie C where we managed to reach the play offs of the season 2020-22 as the second but by God's luck we were promoted following the fact that the rule was amended to promote two teams to series B.
